About


Hello, my name is Lucas Waldburger and I am a student at UC Berkeley conducting research in the Department of Bioengineering. Over the past few years I have had the opportunity to work with several incredible mentors that have enabled me to make the most of my education. I take my work very seriously since I am financially independent and have relied largely on scholarships to pay for college. Nevertheless, I enjoy teaching financially disadvantaged and academically underrepresented students using my native proficiency in Spanish. I also enjoy training for triathlons and recently completed my first marathon. I hope you enjoy my personal website and thank you for visiting!
