About
I am a PhD student in Bioengineering at UC Berkeley advised by Jay Keasling and Patrick Shih.
I am interested in using machine learning and functional genomics to explore biosynthetic diversity and engineer complex functions in living systems. My current research focuses on using high-throughput synthetic biology to decipher natural genetic circuits and design synthetic programs in agriculture and climate applications.
